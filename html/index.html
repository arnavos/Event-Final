Behind the Fest

Creating an Unforgettable Experience

We host a vibrant platform where students from different schools come together to compete, collaborate, and celebrate talent.

  • Our team carefully plans every detail of the fest, from scheduling events to arranging venues, so that everything runs smoothly and participants can enjoy a seamless experience.

  • We organize a variety of competitions that are fun, challenging, and creative, giving students a chance to showcase their talents and compete in a spirit of healthy competition.

  • From interactive workshops to cultural performances, we create exciting activities that let students learn, participate, and make lasting memories with friends and peers.

  • We take care of all logistics, safety, and guidance, making sure every student feels welcomed, comfortable, and enjoys every moment of the fest.

About us

St.Joseph's School

St. Joseph’s School, Greater Noida, established in 2000 by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Agra, is a co-educational senior secondary school affiliated with CISCE. Guided by its motto, “To Work and Serve in Love”, it offers quality education and all-round development, serving students from all communities for nearly 25 years.
