Duo challenge edition.

Blind Strokes

Back

Team Size

2 students (Duo)

Duration

35 minutes per round

Venue

Play Ground

Eligibility

Classes VI-XII

Description

Exploring the world of creative art reveals a thrilling landscape of unconventional techniques, collaborative expression, and trust-building challenges. Here's an electrifying artistic competition you might find captivating:

Instructions

  • • One participant wears a blindfold, the other guides verbally
  • • Switch roles are not allowed during the round
  • • A3 sheets will be provided, students will bring their own colors, pencils and other items
  • • In the final 7 minutes, blindfolds will be removed, and the artists will get a chance to speed-run through their artwork, giving it the finishing touches
  • • Removing blindfold before the given time will result in disqualification

Drawing Methods

  • • Participants may choose between normal drawing (blindfolded), Touch Drawing Method (optional, blindfolded), Blindfolded Imagination, Creative Touch, & Team Work - a thrilling battle of artistic expression!

Participation Details

  • • Eligibility: Group A: Classes VI–VIII, Group B: Classes IX–XII
  • • Team Size: 2 students (Duo only)
  • • Duration: 35 minutes per round
  • • Venue: Play Ground

Evaluation Criteria

  • • Creativity
  • • Time Management and Efforts
  • • Interpretation of theme
  • • Overall Impact
